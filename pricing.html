Rental Pricing

Transparent pricing for your perfect RV adventure. Rates vary by season and rental duration.

Discounts may apply for weekly or long-term rentals. Minimum rental periods may apply during peak season. All prices are indicative and subject to change. Please contact us for a precise quote.

What's Included

  • Standard Vehicle Insurance (deductible applies)
  • Mileage Allowance (e.g., 200 km per rental day included)
  • Basic Kitchen Utensils & Cookware
  • Full Propane Bottle(s) at Pickup
  • Full Fresh Water Tank at Pickup
  • Vehicle Introduction & Operation Guide
  • Roadside Assistance Information
  • Parking for your car during rental (subject to availability)

Optional Add-ons

  • Bedding Kit (duvet, pillow, linens - per person): €25 / rental
  • Towel Set (per person): €10 / rental
  • Upgraded Kitchen Kit (more items, coffee maker): €30 / rental
  • Camping Chairs (per chair): €5 / rental
  • Camping Table: €10 / rental
  • Bike Rack (holds 2-3 bikes): €50 / rental
  • GPS Navigation Unit: €5 / day
  • Child Safety Seat: €20 / rental

Add-ons are subject to availability. Please request when booking.

Important Information

  • Security Deposit: A refundable security deposit (typically €1000 - €1500 depending on RV) is required at pickup via credit card authorization.
  • Driver Requirements: Minimum driver age (e.g., 25 years) and valid driving license held for minimum period (e.g., 2 years). Specific license categories may be required for larger vehicles.
  • Insurance: Standard insurance included has a deductible (excess). Options for reduced deductible may be available.
  • Fuel Policy: Pick up full, return full. Refueling charges apply otherwise.
  • Cleaning: RV must be returned reasonably clean inside. Toilet cassette must be emptied. Cleaning fees apply if requirements are not met (e.g., €100+).
  • Smoking Policy: Strictly non-smoking inside all vehicles.
  • Travel Restrictions: Please inform us of your intended travel destinations. Some country restrictions may apply.
